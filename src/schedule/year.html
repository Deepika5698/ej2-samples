This demo shows the experience of showing the annual year events in a single view with different orientations.

In this demo, we have showcased the Year and TimelineYear views that help to view the appointment in an annual calendar view. The view options are enabled by using the views property. In the TimelineYear, Horizontal and Vertical orientations are available to view the events with a different layout.

Note:- Inject Year and TimelineYear modules for loading these year views.
